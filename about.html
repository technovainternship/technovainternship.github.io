Frequently Asked Questions

Is the internship really free?

Yes! Our virtual internships are 100% free. We believe in democratizing education and providing equal opportunities for all students to learn and grow.

How do I receive the certificate?

Upon successful completion of all assigned tasks within the 4-week period, you will receive a verified certificate via email.

Can I apply for multiple domains?

Yes, but we recommend focusing on one domain at a time to ensure you can deliver high-quality project work.

Is there any selection process?

We review applications on a rolling basis. While we aim to accept most enthusiastic learners, slots are limited for each batch.

Is it work from home?

Absolutely. The entire internship is remote/virtual, allowing you to manage your tasks alongside your college studies.
